Welcome to PawRightSG

What is PawRightSG?

PawRightSG helps families looking to adopt a dog in Singapore narrow down their options based on their current living situation and lifestyle.
Follow 4 simple steps to generate a list of dogs that you might like to meet at a local no-kill shelter!

Who are the dogs featured on PawRightSG?

PawRightSG brings together dogs from three different shelters (more to come!):

  • Action for Singapore Dogs (ASD)

  • Save Our Street Dogs (SOSD)

  • Singapore Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (SPCA)

  • Mercylight Animal Rescue and Sanctuary

  • Oasis Second Chance Animal Shelter (OSCAS)
These dogs are mostly "Singapore Specials" - local-breed street dogs known for being highly intelligent, athletic, and sensitive.

They may have come off the streets because they seem to like people, or from homes that can no longer care for them.
Occassionally, there are also purebreds/crossbreeds that have been given up by breeders, due to age or health issues.


They come in all shapes, sizes and personalities.
But what they have in common, is a desire for a safe space, square meals, and a kind hand to gently pet and protect them.
