Welcome to PawRight

What is Pawright?

Pawright aims to help families looking to adopt a dog in Singapore narrow down their options based on their current living situation and lifestyle.
Answer 5 simple questions to generate a list of dogs that you might like to meet at your local shelter!

Who are the dogs featured on Pawright?

Pawright brings together dogs from three different shelters (more to come!):

  • Action for Singapore Dogs (ASD)

  • Save Our Street Dogs (SOSD)

  • Singapore Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (SPCA)
